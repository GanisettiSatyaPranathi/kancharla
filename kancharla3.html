About Us

Who We Are

The Kancharla Foundation is a non-profit organization founded by Dr. Sreekanth C Kancharla, a visionary leader with a mission to eradicate poverty and uplift rural communities. Driven by this goal, the Foundation implements impactful programs focused on education, health, and sustainable development.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

Our Vision To eradicate poverty and uplift rural communities by empowering individuals through education, healthcare, and sustainable practices. Food Security: Regular food drives for underserved communities. Education: Scholarships and learning programs for children and youth. Healthcare: Organizing medical camps and providing essential health services. Sustainability: Promoting organic farming and environmental conservation.

Vision

Kancharla Foundation is committed to creating a better future for underprivileged communities. With a focus on education, healthcare, and sustainable living, we ensure that every donation makes a tangible impact. Our team works tirelessly to bring positive change and uplift those in need.
